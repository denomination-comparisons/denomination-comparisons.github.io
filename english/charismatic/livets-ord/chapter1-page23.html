Because the Word of God is the sword of the Spirit (Hebrews 4:12), it is an effective weapon against the enemy, Satan. This explains why the Bible is controversial worldwide and people everywhere try to flee from it by twisting or explaining away its contents. Often, they do so by pleading differing Bible interpretations or dismissing its inspiration. Therefore, it is important to know what is meant by the statement "God's Word is inspired."

By 'the inspiration of the Bible' we mean that God, the Holy Spirit, used human authors. He watched over their personalities, literary styles and historical backgrounds to ensure that His revelation to man was written down without error in the original texts.
