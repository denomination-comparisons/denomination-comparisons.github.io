Jesus Believed the Bible

How, then, did Jesus regard the Bible? To answer this, we cannot look at the opinions of various churches, traditions, movements or preachers. Instead we should discover what the Scriptures have to say about themselves and how Jesus viewed them.

About 10 percent of Jesus' daily conversation consisted of quotes from the Old Testament. He cited it in every difficult situation. Here are some examples:

1. When Satan tempted Him in the wilderness (Matthew 4:1-11).

2. When the Pharisees disputed with Him (Matthew 15:1-9).

3. When He argued with his adversaries from the basis of a single word in Scripture (Matthew 22:43-45).
